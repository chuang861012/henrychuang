A simple LINE chatbot built with Ruby on Rails.

### Features

![search](search.png)

Search for Taiwan's statutory law.

-   constitution
-   criminal law
-   civil law

#### All of the data comes from the [Law & Regulations Database of The Republic of China(Taiwan)](https://law.moj.gov.tw/).
